AND Gate

The AND gate outputs 1 only when all inputs are 1. It implements logical conjunction and is one of the fundamental Boolean operations.

Inputs

2+ (typically 2)

Outputs

1 (Y)

Key insight: In CMOS, NAND + inverter is often more efficient than direct AND due to parallel NMOS providing faster pull-down.

Circuit Diagram

#

CMOS Implementation: Direct AND gates are typically built as NAND + Inverter. The parallel PMOS in NAND provides fast pull-up, making NAND-based designs more efficient.

RTL (Verilog / VHDL)

#
Verilog and_gate.v 
module and_gate (
    input  a,
    input  b,
    output y
);
    assign y = a & b;
endmodule
VHDL and_gate.vhd 
library IEEE;
use IEEE.STD_LOGIC_1164.ALL;

entity and_gate is
    port (
        a : in  STD_LOGIC;
        b : in  STD_LOGIC;
        y : out STD_LOGIC
    );
end entity;

architecture dataflow of and_gate is
begin
    y <= a and b;
end architecture;
